About The Project

Our project JOEY is an AI-powered general-purpose chatbot that associates general feelings of loneliness and isolation with mental health ailments

Currently, we all are going through quarantine due to a pandemic covid19 which is spreading at a rapid rate and people are not able to go outside their homes and hence there has been an increase in the number of depression cases worldwide. Loneliness has crept into our lives, disconnecting us from friends and family, and making us increasingly melancholic.

Unfortunately, there is an acute social stigma surrounding therapy and mental health related issues and more often than not, people choose to ignore the telltale signs of these ailments and suffer in silence instead. Also, the patients may be uncomfortable in communicating in-person or a professional consultant may not be available at odd hours.

A Friend You Can Trust

Our utility aims to provide a safe, judgement-free space where you can pour out your thoughts and secrets.

Our Project allows you to create a personalized account at your discretion just by entering in a few details about yourself.

On signing up, a Unique ID will be generated and you can then proceed further and choose your personal chatbot.

Voila! You now have a friend with you 24*7
